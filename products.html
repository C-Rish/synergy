MFin Plus

MFin is a Management Information System for Micro Finance Institutions (Laghu Bitta Sanstha). It is most widely used MIS application among the Mirco financial institutions in Nepal.

Technology has been a great barrier to the growth of MFIs in the country like Nepal. Synergy's MFin has been proved to make technology accessible and cost effective to MFIs throughout Nepal. This has helped in providing banking facilities for the poor which has contributed in the upliftment of their living standard.

The MFIs using MFin has been able to provide better products and servces to their clients. The flexibility and scalability they are achieving from technology has been remarkable. MFin has enabled MFIs in Nepal in running affordable and accessible capital business with timely and accurate operational efficiency. Finally it helps in predicting where the organization will be in the future.
